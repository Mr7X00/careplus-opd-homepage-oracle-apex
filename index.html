Health and environment awareness

Good health begins with daily habits. CarePlus(+) encourages patients to protect their physical health and keep the hospital environment clean, safe, and comfortable for everyone.

View OPD Services
  • Wash hands regularly and follow hygiene practices inside the hospital.
  • Drink clean water, eat balanced food, and avoid unnecessary junk food.
  • Walk daily, sleep well, and visit the doctor for regular checkups.
  • Use dustbins, avoid plastic waste, and support a clean hospital campus.
